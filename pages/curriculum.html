SEMESTER 1

Interface and Interaction Design

objectives

  • Explore the history, applications, and technologies of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI).
  • Foster creativity and innovation in designing and prototyping interfaces and interaction models, emphasizing both graphic and natural dimensions.
  • Apply diverse navigation, content architecture (wireframing), and interaction techniques in Web User Interfaces (WUIs).
  • Utilize computer vision and physical computing techniques to enhance interface design.

Embark on a journey where creativity meets technology, transforming your ability to craft engaging and innovative digital experiences.

Interactive Sound and Image

objectives

  • Understand the synergy between sound and image in dynamic performative environments
  • Acquire essential skills in real-time digital image manipulation, and generation, ensuring proficiency in contemporary technologies.
  • Cultivate skills in interaction design and the effective use of digital interfaces.
  • Create innovative projects that intricately explore the fusion of sound and image, fostering a dynamic and engaging multimedia experience.

Join us in shaping a future where sound and image converge to create immersive experiences.

Motion Graphics and Video Mapping

objectives

  • Comprehend the language and expressive capabilities of graphic animation across platforms.
  • Develop skills in structuring, planning, composing, and animating various graphic elements.
  • Create dynamic narratives through graphic animation.
  • Acquire an understanding of the concepts associated with motion design and video mapping design.
  • Investigate methods for capturing, transforming, and projecting graphic and video animations onto diverse surfaces.

CREATIVE PROGRAMMING

objectives

  • Comprehend fundamental concepts of computer programming in the context of multimedia digital arts.
  • Recognize essential concepts in computational programming, including data types, operators, control structures, and functions.
  • Cultivate creative and critical skills to construct simple, direct, and effective solutions.
  • Integrate creative programming languages as a means of artistic expression and implement programs using open-source integrated development environments.

UBIQUITOUS PLATFORMS | OPTIONAL

objectives

  • Gain a comprehensive understanding of ubiquitous platforms, exploring their definition, challenges, and advantages.
  • Acquire knowledge of how the Web operates
  • Develop the ability to implement basic applications using JavaScript.

CHARACTER ANIMATION IN INTERACTIVE CONTEXTS | OPTIONAL

objectives

  • Acquire a deep understanding of animation concepts and principles.
  • Master the fundamental processes involved in developing, preparing, and animating characters within 3D applications.
  • Grasp the unique demands of character development in diverse contexts, including cinematographic animation, video games, and multimedia products.
